TOUGH LUCK PLAYING CARDS

Every summer our extended family gets together to spend a week playing cards and eating junk food in the secluded woods of northern Minnesota. I look forward to this week all year long. I started this project in my off-time in hopes of giving my family a deck of cards with a little more flavor to play with during that time, and was fortunate enough to collaborate with Legends Playing Card Co. to get them professionally printed.
